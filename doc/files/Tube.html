Tube

Water in the Tube model. It contains a TubeCell, a TubeTimer, and a TubeObserver.

Parameters

  • checkZero: Deny having a negative amount of water in the model.
  • flow: Amount of water that flows out of the tube.
  • initialWater: Initial amount of water in the tube.
  • numRuns: Number of simulation steps of the model.
  • observingStep: Time interval to observe the amount of water along the simulation.
